Stainless Steel Bolting Materials

Stainless steel fasteners offer excellent corrosion resistance, high strength, and durability. Our comprehensive range covers all major stainless steel grades for various applications.

Common Grades

304/304L (A2)

General purpose austenitic stainless steel with excellent corrosion resistance

316/316L (A4)

Marine grade with superior corrosion resistance due to molybdenum content

321 (A3)

Stabilized grade with titanium, excellent high temperature properties

347

Niobium stabilized for high temperature service

310/310S

High temperature oxidation resistance up to 1150¬∞C

317/317L

Enhanced molybdenum for superior chemical resistance
