Duplex & Super Duplex Fasteners

High strength and superior corrosion resistance

Duplex Stainless Steel Fasteners

Duplex and super duplex stainless steels combine the beneficial properties of both austenitic and ferritic stainless steels, offering approximately twice the strength of austenitic stainless steel along with superior corrosion resistance, particularly to stress corrosion cracking and chloride pitting.
