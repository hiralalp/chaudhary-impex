Special Considerations

Titanium: Requires special handling - no galling, use anti-seize, torque carefully

Zirconium/Tantalum: Extremely expensive, used only where essential

Copper Alloys: Excellent for marine - natural antifouling properties

Beryllium Copper: OSHA requirements - toxic dust during machining


Cost Hierarchy (approximate): Tantalum > Zirconium > Titanium > Beryllium Copper > Aluminum Bronze
