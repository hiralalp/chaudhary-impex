ASTM A320 Specification

Alloy Steel and Stainless Steel Bolting for Low Temperature Service

ASTM A320 Overview

ASTM A320 covers alloy steel and stainless steel bolting materials intended for use in low-temperature service and requiring notch toughness testing. This specification ensures fasteners maintain ductility and fracture toughness at cryogenic and sub-zero temperatures.

Installation Considerations

  • Lubrication: Use low-temp compatible anti-seize compounds
  • Thermal Contraction: Account for differential contraction in joint design
  • Preload: May need adjustment for cold assembly vs warm operation
  • Ductile-Brittle Transition: Ensure grade rated below lowest expected temp
  • Gasket Selection: Must also be compatible with service temperature
  • Safety Factor: Typically select grade rated 20-50Â°F below design minimum

Quality Requirements

Material Certification: EN 10204 3.1 or 3.2 certificates required

Heat Treatment: Quench and temper with documented temperature records

Chemical Analysis: Ladle and product analysis required
