About Chaudhary Impex

Our Company

Chaudhary Impex is a leading manufacturer and supplier of exotic alloy fasteners, providing an extensive range of difficult to source, special fasteners and forgings that may require CNC machining to provide complex dimensional shapes.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality fastening solutions for critical applications in aggressive service environments including cryogenic, high temperature service, and conditions involving excessive vibration, impact, or shock.

Our Expertise

We specialize in bolting materials and components with designated compositions and specific properties intended for applications in:

  • Oil & Gas Industry
  • Petrochemical Plants
  • Power Generation
  • Offshore & Subsea Applications
  • Nuclear Power Plants
  • Automotive Industry
  • Aerospace Applications

Quality & Certifications

We are proud to be certified to international standards:

  • ISO 9001:2015 Certified
  • PED 2014/68/EC
  • NACE MR0175/ISO 15156-2
  • NORSOK M-650
  • DFAR Compliant
  • MERKBLATT AD 2000 W2/W7/W10

Our Facilities

Located in Mumbai, India, our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility is equipped with advanced CNC machining capabilities and comprehensive testing equipment to ensure the highest quality standards.
