Design Guidelines

Key Selection Criteria:

  • Parallel Keys: Most common, suitable for most applications
  • Taper Keys: High torque, permanent assemblies
  • Woodruff Keys: Thin sections, self-aligning
  • Multiple Keys: For very high torque applications

Design Considerations:

  • Key length should be 1.5 to 3.0 times shaft diameter
  • Consider stress concentrations at key ends
  • Ensure adequate keyway depth for strength
  • Use chamfers or radii to reduce stress concentrations
  • Material compatibility with shaft and hub
