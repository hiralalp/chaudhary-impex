Design Considerations

Rivet Selection Guidelines:

  • Material Compatibility: Match rivet material to base material
  • Diameter: Typically 3-4 times sheet thickness
  • Length: Grip length + 1.5 x diameter for upset
  • Spacing: Minimum 3 x diameter center-to-center
  • Edge Distance: Minimum 2 x diameter from edge

Joint Strength:

  • Shear strength proportional to rivet cross-sectional area
  • Bearing strength depends on sheet thickness and rivet diameter
  • Multiple rivet patterns distribute load
  • Consider fatigue loading in dynamic applications
