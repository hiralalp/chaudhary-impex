Threaded Rods

Fully threaded rods in various materials and lengths

Product Description

Threaded rods (also known as studs or all-thread) are long rods with continuous threading along their entire length. Used for structural support, hanging applications, and equipment mounting.

Specifications

Standard Lengths

1 meter, 2 meters, 3 meters, and custom lengths available

Thread Types

UNC, UNF, Metric Coarse, Metric Fine

Diameters

M6 to M100 (Metric), 1/4" to 4" (Imperial)

Material Options

Material Specification Features
Carbon Steel Grade B7, Grade 8.8, 10.9 High strength, cost-effective
Stainless Steel 304, 316, 316L Corrosion resistant
Galvanized Steel Hot-dip galvanized Outdoor applications
Duplex Steel S31803, S32205 High strength + corrosion resistance

Applications

  • Structural support and bracing
  • Hanging pipes and equipment
  • Foundation anchoring
  • Equipment mounting
  • Construction projects
  • Seismic retrofitting
