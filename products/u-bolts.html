U-Bolts

Round and square U-bolts for pipe and equipment support

Overview

U-bolts are essential fasteners used for securing pipes, conduit, and other cylindrical objects. Available in various sizes and materials for different applications.

Types

  • Round U-Bolts (for pipe applications)
  • Square U-Bolts (for square tubing)
  • Semi-Round U-Bolts
  • Heavy-Duty U-Bolts
