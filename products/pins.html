Design Considerations

Pin Selection Guidelines:

  • Dowel Pins: Best for precise alignment, permanent assemblies
  • Taper Pins: Self-locking, removable, high shear loads
  • Grooved Pins: Quick assembly, some vibration resistance
  • Spiral Pins: Cost effective, field serviceable
  • Clevis Pins: Hinged connections, removable with tools

Load Considerations:

  • Shear load capacity based on material and diameter
  • Double shear loading preferred over single shear
  • Consider bearing stress on mating parts
  • Safety factor typically 2:1 to 4:1
