Bolting Encyclopedia

TorqBolt provides comprehensive bolting solutions for critical industrial applications. This reference guide covers specifications, standards, materials, and technical data for industrial bolting components including bolts, screws, nuts, washers, and studs.

What is Bolting?

"Bolting" refers to the complete assembly of fastening components used in industrial applications. This includes bolts, screws, nuts, washers, stud bolts, and related hardware manufactured from specialized materials with designated compositions and specific mechanical properties for use in demanding service conditions such as:

  • High temperature and cryogenic environments
  • Corrosive and aggressive chemical service
  • High pressure applications
  • Extreme vibration, impact, or shock conditions
  • Offshore and subsea installations
