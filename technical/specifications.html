ASME & ANSI Standards

ASME B16.5 - Pipe Flanges and Flanged Fittings

Covers pressure-temperature ratings, materials, dimensions, tolerances, marking, testing, and methods of designating openings for pipe flanges and flanged fittings. Available in pressure classes: 150, 300, 600, 900, 1500, 2500.

ASME B18.2.1 - Square, Hex, Heavy Hex and Askew Head Bolts

Dimensional standards for square and hex bolts and screws. Covers nominal sizes from 1/4" through 4" diameter with various thread series (UNC, UNF, 8UN).

ASME B18.2.2 - Square and Hex Nuts

Dimensional requirements for square nuts, hex nuts, hex jam nuts, hex slotted nuts, heavy hex nuts, and heavy hex jam nuts.

Note: Property Class Interpretation

The property class designation (e.g., 8.8) consists of two numbers:

  • First number (8) = 1/100 of nominal tensile strength (800 MPa)
  • Second number (8) = 10 times the ratio of proof stress to tensile strength (8/10 = 0.8)
