ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management

Chaudhary Impex is certified to ISO 9001:2015, demonstrating our commitment to quality management systems that consistently provide products meeting customer and regulatory requirements.

PED 2014/68/EC - Pressure Equipment Directive

Our fasteners comply with the European Pressure Equipment Directive, ensuring safety and quality for pressure-bearing equipment applications.

NACE MR0175 / ISO 15156

We manufacture fasteners compliant with NACE MR0175/ISO 15156 standards for materials used in H2S-containing environments in oil and gas production.

NORSOK M-650

Our products are qualified and compliant with NORSOK M-650 standards for qualification of manufacturers of special bolting materials.

DFAR Compliance

We offer DFAR (Defense Federal Acquisition Regulation) compliant fasteners for defense and aerospace applications, ensuring materials are manufactured in compliance with relevant regulations.
