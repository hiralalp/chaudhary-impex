Molybdenum Disulfide Properties

Why MoS2 is Superior for High Temperature

  • Crystal Structure: Layered hexagonal structure provides easy shear
  • Thermal Stability: Stable to 400Â°C in air, 1100Â°C in vacuum/inert gas
  • Load Capacity: Can support loads up to 250,000 psi
  • Low Friction: Inherent lubricity from crystal structure
  • Dry Film: No liquid carrier, no evaporation or migration
  • Radiation Resistant: Does not degrade under nuclear radiation

Installation Guidelines

  • Torque Reduction: Reduce torque by 30-35% compared to uncoated
  • Lubrication: No additional lubrication required (coating is the lubricant)
  • First Use: Break-in torque may be slightly higher, normalizes on subsequent use
  • Reusability: Excellent - can be reused 5-10 times typically
  • High Temperature: Maintains lubrication even after thermal exposure
  • Storage: Indefinite shelf life, store in dry conditions
  • Inspection: Dark gray/black coating should be uniform and intact
