Offshore & Subsea Bolting Solutions

Offshore and subsea applications demand fasteners with exceptional corrosion resistance, high strength, and reliability in the harshest marine environments. Our comprehensive range meets all offshore industry standards including NORSOK M-650 and DNV requirements.

Corrosion Zones & Material Selection

Zone Environment Recommended Materials
Atmospheric Zone Above splash zone, salt spray 316 SS, Duplex 2205
Splash Zone Wave impact, highest corrosion Super Duplex 2507, 6Mo (254 SMO)
Tidal Zone Alternating wet/dry cycles Super Duplex 2507, Zeron 100
Submerged Zone Continuous seawater immersion Duplex 2205, 316 SS
Subsea (Deep Water) High pressure, cold, H2S possible Super Duplex, Inconel 625/718
